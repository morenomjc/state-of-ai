# The State of AI
### April 2026

Where we are, how we got here,
and where things are heading.

---

## Where We Are Today

- **65%** of companies worldwide now use AI tools
- AI writes code, diagnoses diseases, and runs errands
- A new type of AI — **agents** — acts on your behalf
- We went from chatbot to autonomous assistant in **3 years**

---

## A Quick History

- **1950s** — scientists ask: *"Can machines think?"*
- **2012** — AI learns to recognise images better than humans
- **2022** — ChatGPT launches; 100 million users in 60 days
- **2025–26** — AI doesn't just answer — it **acts**

---

## Two Big Eras of AI

**Era 1 — Generative AI** *(2022–2024)*
> You ask. It answers.

**Era 2 — Agentic AI** *(2025–now)*
> You give a goal. It figures out the steps and does the work.

---

## What Can AI Create?

- **Writing** — emails, reports, summaries, translations
- **Images** — photos, illustrations, logos from a text description
- **Audio & Video** — voices, music, video clips
- **Code** — software written by AI, reviewed by AI

> All from a simple text prompt.

---

## RAG — Giving AI a Memory

AI models are trained once and then frozen — they don't know what happened yesterday.

**RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** fixes this:

1. Your question arrives
2. AI searches a knowledge base for relevant documents
3. It reads those docs *and then* answers

> Like an open-book exam — the AI can look things up before responding.

Used for: company wikis, customer support, legal research, personal assistants.

---

## What Is an AI Agent?

A **chatbot** waits for your next question.

An **agent** goes off and *does things* for you.

- Searches the web
- Reads documents
- Sends emails
- Writes and runs code

> Like having an intern that works 24/7 and never forgets.

---

## Skills — Teaching Agents New Tricks

Agents can be given **skills** — pre-built abilities you can switch on.

- `/summarise` — condense any document
- `/review` — check code or writing for problems
- `/plan` — break a big goal into steps

Think of skills like **apps on your phone** — but for your AI assistant.

---

## Coding Agents — AI That Builds Software

AI can now write entire features, not just single lines.

- **Claude Code** — reads your whole project, makes changes, runs tests
- **GitHub Copilot** — built into the tools developers already use
- **Devin** — fully autonomous; sets up its own environment

> Developers go from *writing code* to *reviewing code AI wrote*.

---

## OpenClaw — AI in Your Pocket

A free, open-source agent that runs **on your own computer**.

- Talks to you via **WhatsApp, Telegram, or Signal**
- Works with any AI model (Claude, ChatGPT, Llama…)
- Became one of the most popular GitHub projects ever in weeks
- Community shares "skill files" — new abilities for free

> Your own personal AI that you fully own and control.

---

## What's Coming Next

- AI assistants that **remember you** across months and years
- Multiple AI agents working together as a team
- AI in every professional field — law, medicine, finance, design
- Governments and companies figuring out the **rules**
